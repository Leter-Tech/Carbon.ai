Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to the most common questions about Carbon.ai

How does Carbon.ai calculate my carbon footprint?
Carbon.ai uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your daily activities, purchases, and energy consumption patterns. We combine this data with industry-standard emission factors and real-time environmental data to provide accurate carbon footprint calculations.
Is my data secure with Carbon.ai?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols, and we never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. Our systems are regularly audited for security compliance.
How can I reduce my carbon footprint?
Carbon.ai provides personalized recommendations based on your activity patterns. This includes suggestions for energy-efficient alternatives, sustainable transportation options, and eco-friendly product choices. Our AI continuously learns from your behavior to offer more targeted advice.
What types of activities can I track?
You can track various activities including purchases, food consumption, waste generation, energy usage, transportation, and offset activities. Our AI can automatically categorize most activities, but you can also manually log and categorize specific items.
How can I get support if I need help?
Our support team is available 24/7 through multiple channels. You can reach us via email, live chat, or through our contact form. We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
